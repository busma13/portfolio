About Peter

Hi, my name is Peter. I am a web developer living in Mesa, Arizona. I was introduced to coding when I took my first programming course in high school, but it wasn't until I was in my thirties that the idea of sitting behind a computer for a living became more appealing.

Growing up in the mountains of southern California, I have always had a love of nature and the outdoors. The Tonto National Forest along the Lower Salt River is my go-to place to get away from the city and hike or kayak. And when I can't get away I can always walk out back to my garden, where I especially enjoy growing tomatoes and bell peppers.

My wife and two children enjoy playing board games or solving a puzzle together. We have a board game collection that is bordering on excessive at this point, but they make for a great excuse to spend more time with the family.
